Download Pioneer

We occasionally make alpha releases of Pioneer to showcase recent developments and to get feedback and ideas. We'd love for you to take Pioneer for a spin.  These are unfinished development snapshots, and are lots of fun!

The latest release is Alpha 32, released 8 March 2013.

#### Binary builds

Pre-built copies of the game are available for the following platforms:

* [Windows](http://sourceforge.net/projects/pioneerspacesim/files/alpha/pioneer-alpha32-win32.7z/download) (155.7MB)
* [Linux 32-bit](http://sourceforge.net/projects/pioneerspacesim/files/alpha/pioneer-alpha32-linux32.tar.bz2/download) (161.0MB)
* [Linux 64-bit](http://sourceforge.net/projects/pioneerspacesim/files/alpha/pioneer-alpha32-linux64.tar.bz2/download) (161.2MB)
